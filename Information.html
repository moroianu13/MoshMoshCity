Lost and Found

We understand that losing personal belongings can be a stressful experience, especially at a large event like our festival. We want to assure you that we are taking every measure to reunite lost items with their rightful owners.

Lost and Found Procedures

If you have lost something during the festival, please visit our Lost and Found tent located near the main entrance. The tent will be open during festival hours and for a limited time after the festival ends.

To increase your chances of recovering your lost item, please provide us with the following information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • A detailed description of the item
  • The location where you last saw the item

Unclaimed Items

Items that remain unclaimed after the festival will be donated to charity.

Prevention Tips

To help prevent lost items, we recommend the following:

  • Keep your belongings close to you at all times.
  • If you are bringing a bag, make sure it is securely closed.
  • Write your name and contact information on your belongings.

We appreciate your cooperation in helping to keep the festival a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Thank you for your understanding.
